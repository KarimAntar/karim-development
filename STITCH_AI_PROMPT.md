## 🌐 Responsive Requirements

- Must work on **desktop** (1280px+), **tablet** (768px), and **mobile** (375px)

## ✅ Quality Expectations

- **No generic UI patterns** — this is not a template, it's a bespoke design
- **Premium feel** — every spacing, shadow, and transition should feel intentional
- **Developer-appropriate aesthetic** — must feel right for the tech/software industry
- **Visual hierarchy** — clear distinction between headings, body, labels, and CTAs
- **Micro-interactions** encouraged — hover states, scroll reveals, animated counters
- **Whitespace is your friend** — breathe between sections, don't cram
